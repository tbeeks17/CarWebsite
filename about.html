About the Garage

I grew up turning wrenches on big block Chevys in my dad’s driveway. What started as a weekend hobby turned into a lifelong passion for preserving and improving American muscle. At MuscleCars Garage, we believe every classic has a story, and our job is to make sure it keeps being told on the open road.
