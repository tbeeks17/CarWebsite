Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a restoration take?

A full frame‑off restoration typically takes 12 to 18 months depending on parts availability and the initial condition of the shell.

Do you source parts for the build?

Yes! We handle all parts sourcing, utilizing our network of trusted suppliers to find rare original parts or high‑quality aftermarket components.

Can I bring in my own parts?

Absolutely. If you’ve already started collecting parts for your build, we are happy to evaluate and integrate them into the project.
